Bittensor is a peer-to-peer blockchain that creates a global marketplace for AI. Train, fine-tune, and run models across a network of specialized subnets—rewarded in TAO.

What is Bittensor?

Bittensor runs on Subtensor, a proof-of-stake chain built on Substrate. It acts as a coordination layer for specialized subnets—each one a marketplace for a different kind of digital commodity: conversational AI, image generation, protein folding, financial forecasting, and more.

Miners and validators contribute and verify machine intelligence; the network rewards them with TAO based on a meritocratic system. No single company controls the infrastructure—it’s open, permissionless, and globally distributed.

The TAO token

TAO has a fixed supply of 21 million, similar to Bitcoin. New TAO is created through mining and validation at a decreasing rate. Each token is tied to verified machine intelligence on the network—rewarding producers and validators who make the ecosystem useful.

TAO is the incentive layer: it aligns everyone toward better, more useful AI instead of closed, centralized systems.
